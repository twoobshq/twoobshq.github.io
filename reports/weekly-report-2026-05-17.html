Ecommerce & Conversion
Working

[✅ WIN] Online ATV held at $157 vs $158 LY and beats the $140 forecast, meaning basket size is healthy even as volume scaled 132.4%.
Needs Attention

This week: Audit the checkout funnel in GA4 for this week to identify the single biggest drop-off step, prioritising mobile — fix or A/B test that step before next weekend's traffic peak.
Paid Media
Working

[✅ WIN] CPA dropped to $33 from $58 LY against a $65 forecast — new customer acquisition is running at nearly half the budgeted cost.
Needs Attention
[⚠️ WATCH] All paid spend is concentrated in Meta with no Google activity — zero diversification means any Meta auction volatility or policy issue has full revenue impact.
[⚠️ WATCH] Spend is up 34.3% YoY while sessions grew only 60.1% vs sales growing 132.4% — monitor frequency and CPM trends weekly for early creative fatigue signals.
This week: Pull Meta ad frequency and CPM data for the top 3 creatives this week; if frequency exceeds 3.0 on any adset, rotate in a fresh creative variant before spend efficiency softens.
Retention & Revenue
Working

[✅ WIN] Returning customers drove 40.6% of online orders at $157 ATV, anchoring revenue quality while new customer acquisition scales.
Needs Attention
