Analyst Notes
Ecommerce & Conversion
Working
[✅ WIN] Online CVR hit 2.37% vs 1.62% LY — a 46% improvement year-on-year, well above last year but still 63bps shy of the 3.0% forecast, so there's meaningful upside left to chase.
[✅ WIN] Sessions grew +60.1% to 22,589 while orders grew +133.6%, confirming traffic quality has materially improved — more visitors are arriving with intent.

This week: Run a checkout funnel audit in GA4 for the current week to identify the highest drop-off step and test one friction-reduction change (e.g. guest checkout prompt or shipping threshold nudge) to close the gap to the 3.0% CVR forecast.
Paid Media
Working
[✅ WIN] Meta ROAS hit 4.7x vs a 3.0x forecast and 2.7x LY — spend of $17,721 is generating outsized returns and the channel is clearly in a strong efficiency window.
[✅ WIN] CPA dropped to $33 vs $58 LY and a $65 forecast — cost to acquire is less than half of what was budgeted, giving significant room to scale spend without blowing efficiency.
[⚠️ WATCH] 100% of paid spend is concentrated in Meta with no Google activity — strong results now, but single-channel dependency is a risk if creative fatigue or an auction shift hits mid-week.
This week: With CPA at $33 against a $65 forecast, test a controlled 20–30% Meta budget increase this week while creative is performing — and brief the team on at least one Google Performance Max or Shopping campaign to reduce single-channel risk.
Retention & Revenue
Working
[✅ WIN] Return rate improved to 5.1% from 6.5% LY on significantly higher volume ($4,828 returns vs $2,759 LY) — product-market fit and sizing confidence appear to be strengthening.
