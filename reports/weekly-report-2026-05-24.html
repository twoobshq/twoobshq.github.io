Ecommerce & Conversion
Working

[✅ WIN] Online ATV of $161 is running $21 ahead of forecast ($140) and up from $146 LY, suggesting customers are buying more per visit.
[⚠️ WATCH] CVR gap to forecast (2.28% vs 3.0% target) represents ~150 missing orders this week — checkout or PDP friction worth investigating given the high new customer mix (70.2%).
Needs Attention
[⚠️ WATCH] New customer share jumped to 70.2% vs 51.8% LY and above the 65% forecast — great acquisition momentum but retention pipeline needs feeding now to protect future revenue.
[⚠️ WATCH] The OG and The OG+ are sitting on 43–50 weeks of cover at current sell-through rates, signalling potential markdown or promotional pressure ahead.
This week: Run a quick checkout funnel audit in GA4 this week to pinpoint the exact step where new visitors are dropping — with 70% new customers and CVR still 72bps below forecast, fixing one friction point could unlock significant incremental revenue.
Paid Media
Working

[✅ WIN] CPA dropped to $32 vs a $65 forecast and $48 LY, meaning you're acquiring customers at roughly half the expected cost — a strong green light to scale cautiously.

This week: While Meta efficiency is peak, stress-test the ceiling by increasing daily budget 20–30% on your best-performing BFF+ creative this week and monitor frequency — if frequency creeps above 3.5, creative fatigue is likely arriving soon.
Retention & Revenue
Working
